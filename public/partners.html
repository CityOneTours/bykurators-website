Anchor Partner
Aum Architects
Mumbai, India · Founded 2004
Design Philosophy

Aum Architects builds with restraint. Over two decades of practice in Mumbai, the studio has developed a design language grounded in material honesty, spatial clarity, and a distinctly Indian sensibility that reads as entirely contemporary. Their work doesn't announce itself — it reveals itself. Spaces that are calm on entry and increasingly considered the longer you spend in them.

Project Types

The studio works across residential and commercial typologies, with particular depth in projects that require a considered relationship between architecture, interior, and landscape. Private residences, boutique commercial spaces, and hospitality environments form the core of their portfolio.
