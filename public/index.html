Our Conviction

"There's a certain kind of space that stops you. Not because it's showy — because everything in it feels like it was placed with purpose."

By Kurators was built on a conviction: that great design is as much about who shapes a space as how. We are based in Dubai and work across the Middle East and East Africa — bringing clients the best of India's design world, and beyond.

Our roster is anchored in the Indian design tradition, a heritage rich enough to anchor any room. But we curate globally, because great design doesn't stop at one border.

We make introductions that change outcomes. And then we stay in the room until the job is done.

01

Design Curation

Finding the right design studio for a project is harder than most people think. Fit matters — aesthetically, temperamentally, sometimes geographically. Our roster starts with India's most accomplished architects and interior designers. From there, we curate outward: practices from across the world whose work meets the same standard.

We match residential homeowners, hospitality groups, and developers with studios whose work and working style genuinely suits them. Based in Dubai, we serve clients throughout the Middle East and East Africa.

02

Project Management Consultancy

Even the best-designed project can unravel without the right person watching over it. By Kurators provides end-to-end project management for interiors and architecture — from scoping and vendor selection through to site supervision and handover.

Our approach is straightforward: clear milestones, honest timelines, and a single point of accountability. No surprises where they can be avoided. And when they can't, a plan ready before the conversation.

The Process

How It
Works

We keep it simple. You tell us what you're building, where you are in the process, and what you need.

Whether you come to us at the ideas stage or mid-chaos, there's usually a way in. We listen — properly. Then we put together a path that makes sense for your project, your timeline, and your budget.

1
Tell us about your project
