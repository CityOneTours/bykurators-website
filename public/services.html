Service 01

Design Curation

The search for the right design studio is one of the most consequential decisions in any project. Get it right, and everything that follows has a foundation. Get it wrong, and no amount of budget or good intention fixes it later.

This is the work By Kurators was built to do. We maintain a carefully curated roster of architecture and interior design studios — practices we know well, trust completely, and match with genuine care. Our clients don't wade through directories or send cold enquiries. They come to us, and we make the right introduction.

We don't send a list. We make a recommendation — and we stand behind it.

A Roster Built on Discernment

Our starting point is India — a design world we know intimately, with a talent pool that is deep, diverse, and increasingly global in its reach. Alongside our India-based partners, we work with exceptional studios from around the world: practices chosen not for their geography but for their quality, their character, and their ability to deliver across borders.

Every studio on our roster has been assessed on three things: the quality of the work, the integrity of the process, and the fit with the kinds of clients and projects we serve.

Who This Service Is For

  • Residential homeowners planning a new build, full renovation, or interior overhaul
  • Hospitality groups and developers who need design partners with cross-regional fluency
  • Clients who have been disappointed by generic results and want studios with a genuine point of view
  • Anyone who values fit — aesthetically, temperamentally, and practically — as much as portfolio credentials
The Process
01
Tell us about your project

A conversation — in person, by phone, or over email. We want to understand the space, the brief, the budget range, and the timeline. And we want to understand you: your aesthetic instincts and what a good outcome looks like.

02
We shortlist and recommend

Based on that conversation, we identify one to three studios from our roster whose work, working style, and project experience genuinely suit your brief. We don't overwhelm — we make a considered recommendation and explain our thinking.

03
We make the introduction

We introduce you to the studio directly — not a cold handover, but a warm and well-prepared connection. We brief the studio on your project and expectations, and we make sure the first meeting is a good one.

04
We stay close

Once the relationship is established, we remain available to both parties. If something needs to be clarified, renegotiated, or redirected, we are there to help. The introduction is the beginning, not the end of our involvement.

Service 02

Project Management
Consultancy

The right studio is the foundation. But a great design can still fall short if the project itself isn't managed well. Our project management consultancy practice exists to close that gap — sitting between client and creative team as a trusted, independent voice that protects the design intent and keeps the process honest.

We don't just manage the process. We protect what the project was supposed to be.

Pre-Construction

  • Brief development and scope definition
  • Budget setting and cost benchmarking
  • Contractor and vendor selection and due diligence
  • Programme and milestone planning

Construction & Implementation

  • Site supervision and quality control
  • Progress tracking against programme and budget
  • Variation management and change control
  • Issue resolution and decision escalation

Completion & Handover

  • Snagging and defect management
  • Final inspections and sign-off coordination
  • Handover documentation and warranties
How We Work
01
Project Initiation

We establish scope, budget parameters, key stakeholders, and success criteria. We review existing documentation and identify gaps or risks early.

02
Procurement & Planning

We lead or advise on selection of contractors and key suppliers. We build the project programme and establish the reporting framework.

03
Delivery Oversight

We are present and accountable throughout the build — on site, in coordination meetings, and in ongoing communication with the client.

04
Completion & Close-Out

We manage the final stages with the same rigour as the build: snagging, defect resolution, handover documentation, and a structured close-out.
